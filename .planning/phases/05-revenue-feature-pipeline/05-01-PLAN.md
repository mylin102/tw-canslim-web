  <behavior>
    - Given a dataframe with at least 15 months of revenue:
      - `rev_yoy` (Year-on-Year) = (current / same-month-last-year) - 1
      - `rev_mom` (Month-on-Month) = (current / previous-month) - 1
      - `rev_acc_1` (Acceleration 1) = `rev_yoy_now - rev_yoy_prev`
      - `rev_acc_2` (Acceleration 2) = `rev_yoy_prev - rev_yoy_prev2`
      - `revenue_score`: +1 for yoy > 0.25, +1 for yoy > 0.50, +1 for mom > 0, +1 for mom > 0.1, +1 for acc_1 > 0, +1 for acc_2 > 0.
      - `rev_accelerating`: boolean, `rev_yoy_now > rev_yoy_prev > rev_yoy_prev2`
      - `rev_strong`: boolean, `rev_yoy > 0.3 and rev_mom > 0.1`
  </behavior>
  <action>

      - Sort by date.
      - Perform calculations using pandas vector operations or series indexing.
      - Handle cases with fewer than 15 data points (return None or empty dict).
      - Handle division by zero or NaN.

      - Implement the +1 logic from the spec.
